How to Upgrade

  1. Research compatible components
  2. Backup your data
  3. Power off your PC and disconnect all cables
  4. Open your PC case
  5. Install new components carefully
  6. Close the PC case and reconnect all cables
  7. Power on your PC and test the upgrades
